🧹 Step 5: Preprocessing Pipeline

1. Create X & Y — X: columns [model, year, transmission, mileage, fuelType, tax, mpg, engineSize] | Y: price
2. Label Encoding — Applied on model (col 0) and fuelType (col -4)
3. One-Hot Encoding — Applied on transmission (col 2) via ColumnTransformer
4. StandardScaler — Feature Standardization (mean=0, std=1)
5. Train-Test Split — 80% Train / 20% Test, random_state=0

X_train: (8534, 12)  |  X_test: (2134, 12)
Y_train: (8534, 1)   |  Y_test: (2134, 1)
